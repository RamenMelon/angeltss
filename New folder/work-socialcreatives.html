Portfolio | Social Creatives Logo Redesign

Graphic Design

School Project

With a passion for digital design and creative work, I eagerly embraced this project. My prior experience and hands-on familiarity with Adobe Illustrator during my Polytechnic days made me well-equipped to utilize my design skills effectively.

The objective of this project was to revamp the logo for Social Creatives, an organization that embodies the non-profit arts social business promoting the connection between art and the human spirit. The module underscored the significance of a well-crafted logo design in establishing a brand's identity and resonating with the community it serves. This experience highlighted the critical role of a compelling logo design in effective branding, capturing the essence of the organization's mission and values.
