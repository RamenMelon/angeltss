Graphic Design

School Project

I faced some challenges while working on this project, which involved creating an interactive Playmax panel design with a strong focus on optimizing the customer experience. Additionally, I learned about the significance of Fibonacci's golden ratio in great designs, which was a requirement for this project. Initially, I struggled to understand how to arrange my design elements according to the golden ratio. However, I sought guidance from my lecturer and researched design examples that utilized this concept. Furthermore, my lecturer taught me new techniques for Adobe Illustrator, providing me with valuable tools to enhance my graphic design and illustration skills.
