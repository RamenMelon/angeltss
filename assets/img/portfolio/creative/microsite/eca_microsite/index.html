Food Festival '2020

June 1 - 28, 2020. Singapore. The Grandstand.

Why Celebrate?

The Hawker Food Festival (HFF) arrives this June with the theme "Savour Singapore in Every Bite". HFF 2020 will honour generations of food heritage and showcase culinary creations that push the envelope on Singaporean cuisine. It celebrates Singapore flavours, amazing talent and the rich depth of our culinary past and future.

Look forward to four weekends of feasting, a variety of exciting programmes, as well as immersive experiences showcasing the evolution of Singapore's cultural tapestry.

Have a Hawkable Time!

HFF 2020 brings the feasting and festivities to the next level.
