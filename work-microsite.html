Web Design

School Project

This microsite, developed for my End of Course Assessment, utilizes HTML, CSS, bootstrap, and various JavaScript libraries (Easel.js, Tween.js, Sound.js, Stellar.js, jQuery) for an interactive experience.

With a "Festival" theme, specifically the Hawker Food Festival, the microsite aims to inform and attract visitors, showcasing Singapore's diverse flavors and cultural talent. Organized by SGconnect, this one-time event spans four weekends (1st to 28th June 2020) at The Grandstand Singapore, celebrating the rich culinary heritage of Singapore.
